Company

Care Health Insurance

Care Health Insurance, formerly known as Religare Health Insurance, is a specialised health insurer now in its 13th year of operation. It offers individual, family and senior citizen health cover, designed around real-world claim scenarios rather than just policy paperwork.

13+ Years as a dedicated health insurer
22,000+ Healthcare providers in network
₹16,000+ Cr Claims paid since inception
24/7 Claim support, every day

Its cashless network means eligible hospital bills are settled directly with the hospital, so a policyholder isn't stuck arranging funds during a medical emergency. For care outside the network, reimbursement claims are handled through the same dedicated claims team.

Plans, by who they're built for

What's on offer

Health cover isn't one-size-fits-all — plans are built around a person's age, family situation and specific risk. Here's the basic breakdown Shyamu can walk you through.

For one person

Individual Health Insurance

A standalone policy sized to one person's own medical needs and budget.

For families

Family Floater

One sum insured, shared across the whole family under a single policy.

For senior citizens

Senior Citizen Health Insurance

Built for higher-age enrolment, with cover suited to age-related conditions.

For specific risk

Critical Illness Insurance

A lump-sum payout on diagnosis of a specified serious illness, on top of regular cover.

For existing policyholders

Super Top-up

Extra cover layered on top of an existing base policy, for a lower additional premium.

For employers

Corporate & Group Insurance

Health and personal accident cover an employer extends to its own team.
